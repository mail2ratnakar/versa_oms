# Mobile Responsive Rules

## Priority

School portal must work well on mobile.

Staff portal must support basic review on mobile but heavy operations may be desktop-first.

## Breakpoints

- Mobile: < 640px.
- Tablet: 640px–1024px.
- Desktop: > 1024px.

## Mobile Behavior

- Sidebar collapses to drawer or bottom nav.
- Tables convert to cards for school portal.
- Staff tables may use horizontal scroll with sticky first column.
- Primary actions move to bottom sticky bar only when useful.
- Forms become single column.
- Modals use full-screen sheet on mobile.
- Tap targets minimum 44px.

## Avoid

- Dense tables on school mobile.
- Tiny badges.
- Multi-column forms.
- Hidden critical actions.
